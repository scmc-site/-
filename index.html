WELCOME TO SO-CAL MANGA CLUB

We’re a friendly group of manga fans in Southern California who share recommendations, talk about our favorite stories, and explore all kinds of manga. We also organize fun outdoor events like hiking trips, convention center visits, and other exciting activities. Whether you’ve been reading manga for years or are just curious, this is the perfect place to connect, learn, and have fun with fellow fans!
