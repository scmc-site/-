WHO WE ARE

We’re a group of manga fans from Southern California who love reading, sharing, and talking about manga. Whether you're new to manga or have been a fan for years, you'll feel at home here. We enjoy discovering new series, giving recommendations, and discussing our favorite stories. We also plan fun outdoor events, meet-ups, and trips to conventions. Our goal is to create a friendly space where everyone can connect, learn, and have fun together!

WHAT BUILT US

We came together because of our shared love for manga and anime. What started as a small group of friends has grown into a welcoming community. Our club is built on friendship, respect, and fun, where everyone can share their love for manga and feel like family. We believe in creating a place where all fans can connect and enjoy the things they love.

WHAT WE VALUE

We value friendship, respect, and welcoming everyone. No matter how much you know about manga, you’re always welcome here. We believe in creating a supportive space where people can share ideas, learn from each other, and enjoy manga together. Most importantly, we value having fun and making sure everyone feels at home.
